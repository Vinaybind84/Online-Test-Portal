About Portal

Welcome to the Softpro Online Test Portal, a powerful platform to make online test management seamless and efficient. This portal is specifically designed to help Softpro India assess and evaluate students on a large scale, ultimately aiding in the selection process for our free internship programs. By leveraging this portal, we can sort students based on their test performance and identify top talent for valuable internship opportunities.

The portal features a user-friendly interface that ensures a smooth and intuitive experience for both students and administrators, making it easy to create, manage, and evaluate tests. Having already facilitated assessments for over 17,000 students, the platform has proven to be highly reliable, scalable, and efficient, allowing us to handle large volumes of test-takers effortlessly.

Through this portal, we aim to continue identifying bright minds and providing them with meaningful learning opportunities at Softpro India.
